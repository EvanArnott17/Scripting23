About

Loon Lake is a tiny village located in the northwest area of Saskatchewan. Being a village Loon Lake is home to just under 300 people. However, being out in the middle of nowhere makes Loon Lake a peaceful place to live. It is the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of the city. In Loon Lake you are always surrounded by nature.
