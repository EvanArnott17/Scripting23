Loon Lake as a village has an extremely old population. Most of the people that live in the village are in their 50s or older. The old age can also be a problem for many residents, because Loon Lake no longer has a functioning hospital, so in an emergency people need to drive 40 minutes to get access to one. The old average age of the village probably exists because, if people want to do something other than farming, or teaching they are forced to move elsewhere.
