Lay of the land

Like most places in Saskatchewan the land around Loon Lake is quite flat. Much of the land starting from just outside of town is farmland that goes on for many kilometers. Just outside of the village there is a creek, and in winter certain places around the creek turn into great sledding hills. About a 15-minute drive outside of Loon Lake you can find a couple of different lakes. The two main ones are Makwa Lake and Jumbo Lake. In the winter when the lakes freeze over many of the people go out ice fishing.
