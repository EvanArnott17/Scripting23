Summer

In the winter there are a variety of things to do in Loon lake. Some of the inhabitants choose to go ice fishing on one of the lakes located just outside of Loon Lake. Many People also have snowmobiles that they can take out for a ride during the winter. On top of that there are a couple of places located around Loon Lake that turn into good sledding hills.

In the summer there are many things to do in Loon Lake. The main attraction is the couple of diferent campgrounds located a short distance outside of the village. The campground offers plenty of oppertunity for families to spend time boating, or at the beach. Another attraction that Loon lake has is the local 9 hole golf course. It is extremely well maintained and one of the nicest courses in northern Saskatchewan.
